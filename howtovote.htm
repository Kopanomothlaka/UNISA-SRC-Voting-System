How to Vote

Follow the steps below to cast your vote successfully:

Step 1: Register or Log In

Make sure you're logged into your account or register if you're a new user. You'll need an active account to cast your vote.

Click here to Log In/Register

Step 2: Choose an Election

Once you're logged in, select the election you wish to vote in from the available options. You can find a list of elections on the voting page.

Go to Election Page

Step 3: Cast Your Vote

Once you've selected an election, choose the candidate or party you want to vote for. Click the 'Vote' button to submit your vote.

Proceed to Vote

Step 4: OTP Verification

After clicking the 'Vote' button, you will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on your phone. Enter the OTP in the input field below to complete your voting process.

Entering OTP

Step 5: Confirmation

Once the correct OTP is entered, your vote will be confirmed. Thank you for participating in the election!
